## **2. Why Two Volumes Required**

ONTAP ke andar **volume ‚â† AWS EBS volume**.  
Yaha ‚Äúvolume‚Äù ka matlab hai **logical storage container inside ONTAP** (think like folders but more powerful).

AWS ONTAP setup me do volumes isliye hoti hain:
1. **Root Volume (System Volume)**
    - Yeh ONTAP ke apne configuration, metadata, and internal data ke liye hota hai.
    - Isme tum apna user data store nahi karte.

2. **User / Data Volume**
    - Yeh wo jagah hai jaha tumhari files, DB data, backups store hote hain.
    - Tumhare workloads ke liye hota hai.

üëâ Root volume = brain,
